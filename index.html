About Us

Welcome To Wah! Marathi - Cuisine from Maharashtra on your platter

Through Wah! Marathi we intend to honor the cuisine that has its roots spread deep into the Great Maharashtrian heritage. The underscore of “WAH! MARATHI” remains the assortment of delicacies from all over Maharashtra presented in a vogue ambience with contemporary elements. The range of our menu is compiled from each sect of the culture and is as diverse, comprehensive and sumptuous as the Maharashtrian culture in itself is! The stylish restaurant has come up with an all-extensive menu to tickle the ever-demanding taste buds of the connoisseurs with a selective range of dishes and desserts. We have ensured that you surely find your favorite preparation in our range in its pure essence. The delicacies will set you through nostalgia and make your heart say, Wah! Marathi…
